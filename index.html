Puritan Theology and the Witch

Day before the Nones of April MMXXVI

My first video! Based off a paper I did in undergrad on Tituba, an enslaved woman owned by Samuel Parris, and the first person to be accused of witchcraft in Salem Village in 1692. I give a brief overview of what the Salem Witch Trials were, what the Puritans believed, and how religious, cultural, and political forces combined to create a horrific tragedy.
